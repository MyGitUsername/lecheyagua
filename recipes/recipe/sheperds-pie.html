Sheperd's Pie (V GF)

Ingredients

Filling
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 leek finely chopped
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 lb. mushrooms variety finely chopped
  • 5 oz. soyrizo
  • 1/2 cup parsley finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tbps. soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbps. mushroom powder
  • salt and black pepper to taste
Mashed Potatoe Layer
  • 6 medium yellow potatoes
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 tsp. marjoram
  • vegetable oil
